# instagram/approved/

Human-approved Instagram visuals. Passed feed-context test. Ready for manual publishing.

## What belongs here

- Final visual candidate that passed human review
- Feed-tested (checked at mobile render size, both square crop and feed scroll)
- Paired with an approved caption

## What does NOT belong here

- Raw or untested variants (those stay in raw/)
- Visuals that have already been deployed (those move to deployed/)
- LinkedIn or Facebook visuals

## When files move out

Move to `deployed/` immediately after the post goes live.
If a visual is approved but never used, it stays here with a note explaining why.

## Notes to accompany the asset

```
2026-05-21_instagram_agent-loopovi_APPROVED.png

Approved: YYYY-MM-DD
Caption: blog-001_instagram_APPROVED.md (V2)
Category: [A/B/C/D from VISUAL_RULES.md]
Base: [color], accent: [color]
Feed-tested: yes — mobile square + portrait crop
Notes: [any corrections from raw version]
```

## Instagram-specific

Approved visual must have been tested in actual Instagram feed context — not just Canva or Figma preview.
Check both the grid view (smaller) and the feed scroll size.
Verify text is readable before the post is tapped/expanded.
